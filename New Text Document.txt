Here is a complete text summary of **Anil Neerukonda Institute of Technology & Sciences (ANITS)** based on a wide range of current web resources:

***

**Overview & History**
- Established in 2001–02 by Anil Neerukonda Educational Society (ANES) in memory of Dr. N.B.R. Prasad[1][2][3]
- Private, autonomous institution affiliated to Andhra University, Visakhapatnam; approved by AICTE and UGC[4][1]
- Accredited with NAAC “A+” (till Dec 2019, 3.01 CGPA); NBA accredited for 7 key BTech programs under Tier I[5][1]
- Recognized as a Research Centre; owned by Megha Engineering and Infrastructure Limited (MEIL)[3]

**Location & Contact**
- Campus: Sangivalasa, Bheemunipatnam, Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh, PIN: 531162
- Phone: 08933-225083/84 | Principal: kalvasrk@anits.edu.in | Placement: dean.placements@anits.edu.in | Website: www.anits.edu.in[6][7][8]

**Academic Programs**
- **Undergraduate (BTech, 4 years)**
  - Chemical Engineering
  - Civil Engineering
  - Computer Science & Engineering (CSE)
  - CSE (Data Science), CSE (AI & ML)
  - Electrical & Electronics Engineering (EEE)
  - Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE)
  - Information Technology (IT)
  - Mechanical Engineering[9][10][11][1]

- **Postgraduate (MTech, 2 years; MBA)**
  - Control Systems, Computer Science & Technology
  - Communication Systems, Machine Design
  - Soil Mechanics, Biotechnology[12][1][9]

- **Total Seats:** About 1,725 across branches[12]

**Admissions (2025)**
- **UG (BTech):** Via AP EAMCET; candidates need 10+2 with 60% aggregate; category B (management quota) admissions also available[13][14][9]
- **PG:** Via GATE, AP PGECET[15][9]
- Application: Online through official website[15]
- Regular updates for application and counselling dates[9][15]

**Fees Structure**
- BTech average: ₹76,300/annum (management quota and regular may differ)[13]
- Programs range from ₹70,000 to ₹2,39,800 total[12]
- Annual and semester-wise breakdowns available via the website/office[16]

**Campus Facilities**
- State-of-the-art labs, 20+ specialized laboratories[11]
- Central library with over 50,000 books, 100 journals, digital resources[11]
- Wi-Fi enabled campus, digital classrooms, business incubation center[11][12]
- Medical/hospital, hostels for boys and girls, gym, cafeteria, shuttle service, sports complex, convenience stores[12]
- Transport connectivity to Visakhapatnam city

**Placements & Training**
- Dedicated Training & Placement Cell offering regular campus recruitment training (CRT): Quant, logical, verbal, English, group discussions, interview skills[17][9]
- Top recruiters: Infosys, TCS, Cognizant, Capgemini, Wipro, Virtusa, Accenture etc.[18]
- Median salary: ~₹4.50 lakhs/year (NIRF 2025)[18][12]
- 729 out of 1,164 engineering students placed (latest cycle)[18]

**Key Achievements & Rankings**
- High NIRF/MHRD national ranking[3]
- Multiple awards: AI-level hackathon winner, recognized by AP State Skills Development Corporation[19][5]
- AAA rating by Careers360

**Departments**
- Covers standard engineering branches (CSE, ECE, EEE, MECH, CIVIL, CHEM, IT) and allied sciences; R&D, IQAC, Freshman Engineering[2][10][1][9]

**Other Services**
- Business & entrepreneurship incubation[12]
- Research & consultancy services
- Skill development and professional training programs

**Notable Points**
- Prominent engineering and management institute in Andhra Pradesh, highly rated for academic quality, infrastructure, placements, and research[3][11]
